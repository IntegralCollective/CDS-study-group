# Contributing & Governance

Thank you for your interest in the Integral CDS Study Group.

## Guiding Principles

* Collaborate openly and respectfully.
* Prioritize clarity and accuracy.
* Distinguish between summaries, explanations, and personal interpretations.
* Discuss significant changes before implementing them.

## How to Contribute

You can contribute by:

* Initiating summaries of modules not yet summarized
* Improving pre-existing module summaries
* Creating or refining Mermaid diagrams
* Correcting errors
* Asking questions
* Participating in discussions
* Reviewing pull requests

All contributions are welcome—not just code.

## Pull Requests

* Keep pull requests focused on a single topic when possible.
* Explain what changed and why.
* Be open to feedback.
* Significant changes should be discussed first through an Issue or Discussion.

## Governance

This repository is community-governed.

Organization owners are responsible for maintaining the GitHub organization and repository infrastructure. They are custodians of the project, not its leaders.

Project direction is developed through open discussion and community consensus whenever possible. Pull requests are reviewed on the basis of accuracy, clarity, and consistency rather than the status of the contributor.

As the project grows, governance may evolve through the same open and collaborative process used for all major repository decisions.
