# Glossary

## Decision

The synthesized outcome of the deliberation pipeline.

## Issue

A decision to be resolved. It contains one or more [submissions](#submission).

## Participant

A person with identity, role context, and decision weight.

## Scenario

One possible solution path.

## Submission

Any proposal, objection, evidence, comment, proposed by a [participant](#participant) or incoming from a system signal. Submissions are part of an [issue](#issue).

## Votes and Objections

Encode gradient preference and principled resistance.
